COLLATERAL MONITORING SYSTEM for managing Wholesale Banking Book Collateral to mitigate risks involved in varied lines of business of a global bank. Collateral System will allow managing and monitoring the collaterals positions with respect to eligibility checks, concentration limits, surplus or shortfalls based on pledge agreements. Collateral System will also provide the opportunity to eliminate, wherever possible, manual interventions and automate those manual processes.
Designed and developed business logic for file upload processing, file parsing, and validation utilities using Java, J2EE technologies.

Various Technologies Used :

Java, spring, Hibernate, ExtJS, Unix, WebSphere, Tomcat, Oracle, SVN Source control, JIRA, Quality Center, iProcess
